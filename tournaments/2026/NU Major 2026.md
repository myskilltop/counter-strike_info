# NU Major 2026 (Nazarbayev University • Online + LAN Finals • Astana)

🔥 **Prize Pool — 1,000,000 KZT**
**NU Major 2026** is a **Nazarbayev University student tournament** featuring a **Double Elimination** format and **LAN Finals** held at the university.

---

# ⚠️ IMPORTANT: ONLY CURRENT NAZARBAYEV UNIVERSITY STUDENTS ARE ELIGIBLE

**Bachelor's and Master's students only.**

---

## 📅 Schedule

* **Tournament dates:** **April 11–18, 2026**
* **LAN Finals:** **April 18**
* **Online stage:** open registration and Play-off
* **LAN stage:** final matches at **Nazarbayev University**, **Astana, Kazakhstan**

---

## ⚔️ Format

### 🔴 Play-off

* **16 teams** advance from open registration
* Teams are placed in the bracket via **random draw**
* **Format:** **Double Elimination Bracket**
* **Opening Matches:** **BO1**
* **All remaining matches:** **BO3**
* The online stage determines the **top 3 teams**, who advance to the **LAN Finals**

### 🏆 LAN Finals

* **3 teams** continue the final stage on LAN
* **Lower Bracket Final:** **BO3**
* **Grand Final:** **BO3**
